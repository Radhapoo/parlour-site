Our Story

Polar Bear is home to extraordinary Sundaes crafted with love and perfection. Just a decade ago it all started... our passion for ice-creams made us the preferred brand of the generation next. Amazing products and world-class procedures coupled with innovation have made us the fastest growing ice-cream chain in India.

Our Mission

Polar Bear, we don’t believe in compromise. We believe in living life to the fullest and offer outrageously good treats that taste amazing and are good for everyone. Equally important to our commitment to innovation is our promise of quality. Only best ingredients sourced globally are used in making our perfect Sundaes.

Our Presence

Polar Bear has continued to grow year on year into a serious contender for the ice-cream sundaes. Jump forward to 2019 and we have a rapidly expanding store network across the country. 78 Polar Bear flagship stores and 15 Polar Bear Cubs kiosks provide world-class environment to our customers. Bengaluru, Mysuru, Kolar, Hubli, Udipi, Kunigal, Maddur, Mandya, Coimbatore, Salem, Hosur, Davangere and Pune are some cities that have tasted the sweetness of Polar Bear.

With a whopping 83 sundae zones and more, we provide ultimate icecream experience to the world.
